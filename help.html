Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, navigate to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

How do I update my profile information?

You can update your profile information from the "Account Overview" section. Navigate to "Settings" and update your personal details.

Who do I contact for technical support?

If you are facing technical issues, please reach out to our support team by sending an email to support@yourapp.com.
